Use Energy Saving Features To Optimise The Power Consumption Of Devices

What Benefits Does This Have?

Energy demand is heavily reduced, which allows for smaller renewable energy sources to replace the current unrenewable sources. Because of this change, carbon dioxide emmisions will drop, and many coal (and other matirials that are used) mines will cease operation. Additionally, the lower energy demand results in a more stable power supply, and the opperating costs decrease along with the amount of money required to buy the electricity.
