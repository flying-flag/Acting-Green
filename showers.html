Reduce The Amount Of Time Spent In The Shower

What benifits does this have?

Taking shorter showers not only uses less water, but also saves power on heating up the water. This also saves money that would've been used to buy the resources, and allows for a more stable water and electricity services because of the lower demand. Furthermore, it is easier for companies to provide these services with the lower demand, and you can share showers with your family without them having to wait as long.
